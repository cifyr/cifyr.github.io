Our Mission

WheelU is WashU's community for personal mobility enthusiasts — from onewheels and e-bikes to scooters, skateboards, and more.

Our mission is to build community through group rides, safety education, and advocacy for better mobility access on campus.

Ready to Roll With Us?

Whether you're a seasoned rider or just getting started, we welcome all skill levels. Join our community to stay updated on rides, events, and mobility news at WashU.
